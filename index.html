I am currently a researcher in the Semantic Media Technologies group at Fraunhofer IDMT.

My work has generally concentrated on many aspects of music information retrieval, including transcription, chord detection, beat tracking, as well as symbolic music processing and modelling, among others. More recently, I have also focused on generative AI, including the development of tools for musicians, the fair attribution of money for training data, and the detection of AI generated outputs.

Use the navigation buttons on the left to find out more about my work, or click here (or on the link at the bottom of each page) to see my CV.
